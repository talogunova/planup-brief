The Direction
The "Food" Question

What do people always do, no matter what? Eat, sleep, walk. So what could be "food" for AEC specialists? A product or a service they will need on a daily basis for the next 10 to 20 years.

Compliance validation plugin (or plugins)
BIM Health Cleaner and Consulting
Generative Layout Optimization
Anything else?
The Direction
Three Conditions Test
a It has to be something every firm does
b It has to repeat on every project or on a fixed cycle, not once
c It has to be something they cannot choose to skip, either because the law requires it or because skipping it creates liability they cannot accept

Only the first one passes all three conditions cleanly: compliance validation. Every project. Every jurisdiction. Every revision cycle. It cannot be skipped because the architect's stamp carries legal liability.
